Design:
	* PYTHON2.7
	* Compares two images and ranks them on similarity scale

Dependencies:
	* PIL
	* urllib2
	
Usage:

	* Enter the links to the images
	* sample:
		Enter URL of Image1 : http://www.aparisguide.com/eiffeltower/eiffel-tower1.jpg
		Enter URL of Image2 : http://www.earthinpictures.com/world/france/paris/eiffel_tower.jpg
